Boggle (Static)
How to Play
  • Built for a shared screen -- everyone plays from the same board at once.
  • Find words out loud or on paper; there's no on-screen word entry.
  • Words must be at least 3 letters. "Qu" counts as a single letter.
  • When time runs out (or you press End Game in Untimed mode), every possible word on the board is revealed.
