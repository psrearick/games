Boggle Rules

How to Use This Page

  • Choose a board size and time limit (or switch to Untimed), then press Start Game.
  • Drag across adjacent letters, or tap them one at a time, to spell a word.
  • Tap the last letter again, or press Enter, to submit the word. Press Escape or Backspace to clear or undo your selection.
  • The HUD shows time remaining (or ∞ if untimed), your score, and words found.
  • Press End Game any time to stop early — a timed game also ends on its own when the clock runs out.
  • When the game ends, every word the board could have made is revealed, with the ones you found marked.

Full Rules

  • The board is a grid of lettered dice (4×4 "Classic" or 5×5 "Big"), freshly shuffled every game.
  • Build a word by chaining letters that are adjacent to each other, including diagonally. Each die can only be used once per word.
  • Words must be at least 3 letters long. "Qu" on a die counts as one letter, standing in for both Q and U (e.g. "Qu" + "I" + "T" spells QUIT).
  • Only real dictionary words count, checked against the game's built-in word list. Each word only scores once per game.
  • Scoring is by word length: 3–4 letters = 1 point, 5 = 2, 6 = 3, 7 = 5, 8 or more = 11 points.
