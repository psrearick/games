Boggle
How to Play
  • Drag or tap adjacent letters (including diagonals) to build a word.
  • Words must be at least 3 letters. "Qu" counts as a single letter.
  • Each word only counts once per game.
  • Scoring: 3–4 letters = 1 point, 5 = 2, 6 = 3, 7 = 5, 8+ = 11.
  • When the game ends, every possible word on the board is revealed.
