About

These are quiet games.

I have chronic migraines and my mom has vertigo. Between the two of us, most tabletop games and most digital games are off the table. The motion, the flashing, the transitions, the noise, the sheer amount of stuff moving on screen, any one of these can set off a migraine or vertigo episode. We wanted something simple to play together that wouldn't cost us the rest of the day. I couldn't find it, so I built it.

Everything here is made to be gentle on sensitive eyes and heads. Low contrast, no flashing, no animations, no autoplay, minimal motion, and as little visual clutter as I could manage. The idea is that you can sit down and play without bracing for discomfort.

Right now it's a small collection, and I add to it when I can. It's free, and it will stay free. If it helps you and you feel like helping keep it running, there's a link to support me in the footer. If not, that's completely fine. Mostly, I just hope it's useful to someone else who needs screens to be less stimulating.
